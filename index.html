O nás

Stefan Živkovič is a passionate graphic designer and mural artist with over 10 years of experience transforming blank walls into stunning works of art. Specializing in large-scale murals for corporate offices, public spaces, and city beautification projects, Alex combines creativity with precision to deliver unique and inspiring designs.

Whether you want to brighten up your workspace or add cultural vibrancy to your community, Alex’s custom murals and graphic designs will bring your vision to life.

Services

Mural Painting

Custom large-scale murals for city walls, corporate buildings, schools, and public spaces that inspire and engage communities.

Graphic Design

Branding, logos, posters, and digital artwork tailored to your business or project to create a cohesive and professional look.

Wall Art Consultation

Expert advice on mural concepts, color schemes, and materials to ensure your wall art is impactful and long-lasting.
